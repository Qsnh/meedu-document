## 准备条件

- 已通过认证的微信公众号（服务号）

![image.png](https://minio.meedu.vip/meedu-faq/images/b01a0e23da718a08989311b8b2dfb069/pLcHWmY5a4VfFIg6s9fz4ep9jJ4K5wTa.png)

![image.png](https://minio.meedu.vip/meedu-faq/images/b01a0e23da718a08989311b8b2dfb069/KOygeQ3JXH1Sbtw6UlCIcaFPrfFoG588.png)

![image.png](https://minio.meedu.vip/meedu-faq/images/b01a0e23da718a08989311b8b2dfb069/7TxSMomXvtuEsjuZQnwcNtpHCGABDG3A.png)

![image.png](https://minio.meedu.vip/meedu-faq/images/b01a0e23da718a08989311b8b2dfb069/qRYzS00azBB8sC8dQtNsDxjM0EEUS47a.png)
